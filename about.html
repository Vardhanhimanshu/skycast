About SkyCast

Bringing you the future of weather forecasting.

Our Mission

At SkyCast, we believe weather data should be beautiful, accessible, and accurate. We started this project with a simple goal: to replace the boring, cluttered weather apps of the past with a clean, glassmorphism-inspired interface that works instantly on any device.

Why Choose Us?

Auto-Location

Detects your location automatically.

Responsive

Looks great on Mobile, Tablet & Desktop.

The Technology

SkyCast is built using vanilla **HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript**. We utilize the **Open-Meteo API** for weather data, which allows us to provide accurate forecasts without tracking user data or requiring API keys. The background effects are created using pure CSS gradients and backdrop-filters.
